Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

1. What this covers

This policy explains what MacroLoop ("the app," "we") collects, why, and what you can do about it. By creating an account, you agree to this policy.

2. Information we collect

Account information: email address and password (handled via Firebase Authentication — we never see or store your raw password ourselves).

Profile information: sex, age, height, weight, activity level, and your stated goal (lose/maintain/gain weight, and at what rate).

Logged data: food entries (including photos you choose to submit for AI analysis), exercise entries, and weight check-ins over time.

Photos: if you use photo-based food logging, the image is sent to a third-party AI vision model for analysis and is not permanently stored by us beyond what's needed to process that single request.

Usage data: basic app usage (e.g., which features are used) may be collected to understand and improve the app.

We do not collect more than the app needs to function, and we do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. How we use your information

  • To calculate your calorie and macronutrient targets, including the adaptive personalization feature, which uses your logged intake and weight history to refine that target over time.
  • To provide the core features of the app: logging, progress tracking, and (for Premium users) additional analysis.
  • To maintain account security and prevent abuse (for example, rate-limiting how often the photo-analysis feature can be used).

4. Third-party services we use

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication and data storage. Firebase's own privacy practices apply to how they handle data on our behalf.
  • An AI vision model provider (via OpenRouter) — processes photos you submit for food identification. Photos are sent for the purpose of that single analysis.
  • USDA FoodData Central and Open Food Facts — public nutrition databases used for manual food search. Your search terms may be sent to these services, but not your personal profile or logged data.

5. Your rights

You can:

  • Export your data at any time from within the app.
  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time from within the app.

We aim to make both of these genuinely functional, not just a formality — if either doesn't work as expected, that's a bug, not intended behavior.

6. Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data is removed from our active systems; backups (if any) are purged on a rolling basis.

7. Children's privacy

MacroLoop is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children under this age. If you believe a child has created an account, contact us and we will remove it.

8. International users

MacroLoop is operated from Sudan and available worldwide. Your information may be processed in a country other than your own, including wherever our hosting and service providers operate. By using the app, you consent to this transfer.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app changes. Material changes will be reflected here with an updated date.
